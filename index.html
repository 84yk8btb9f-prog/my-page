The problem

Every builder hits the same wall

You finish something. You need honest feedback. Every option available to you fails.

01

Friends are too kind

The people who care about you don't want to hurt your feelings. They say "looks great" instead of telling you what's broken. Polite feedback is useless feedback.

02

Forums give nothing

You post your project online and get ignored, get a one-word reply, or advice from someone who has never built anything like yours. No accountability, no skin in the game.

03

Real feedback costs too much

Hiring a consultant isn't realistic early on. Most founders are left guessing what's wrong until they've already shipped the wrong thing.

Built on trust

Quality by design

Every part of the platform is designed to keep feedback honest, structured, and fair for both sides.

01

Reviewer ratings

Every review is rated after delivery. Public ratings ensure accountability — quality reviewers rise, low-effort ones stop getting matched.

02

Structured feedback

Reviewers work through structured templates. No vague "looks nice" — every piece addresses specific, defined areas of your project.

03

Dispute protection

Clear dispute resolution for both project owners and reviewers. Platform mediation available for any contested feedback.

04

Anti-abuse filtering

Advanced filtering prevents fake reviews and rating manipulation. Identity patterns keep the platform clean for everyone.

05

Transparent profiles

Project owners see reviewer history before matching. Reviewers see clear project briefs before accepting. Every step visible.

06

Quality incentives

Top-rated reviewers get priority access to new projects. The system rewards consistency and penalizes low-effort work.
